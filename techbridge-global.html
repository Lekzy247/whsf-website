WHSF Innovation Project

TechBridge Global

A mobile-first platform connecting African students with technology professionals, mentors, universities, companies, diaspora experts and international volunteers worldwide.

View MVP architecture Partner with WHSF
Vision Global mentorship. Local opportunity.

TechBridge Global is designed to help learners discover career paths, connect with mentors, access training, and apply for technology opportunities across the world.

Core users

Built for learners, mentors and global partners.

The platform creates a bridge between African students and the global technology ecosystem.

01Secondary school students
02University students
03ICT Girls Club members
04TechWomen participants
05African diaspora professionals
06International volunteers

Technology features

A practical platform for mentorship, learning and opportunity.

AI

AI Mentor Matching

Students select their interests and the system recommends relevant mentors abroad based on career goals, skills and availability.

  • Software Engineering
  • AI, Cybersecurity and Cloud Computing
  • Robotics, Data Science and UI/UX
Recommended stack: Flutter, Firebase Firestore and OpenAI-powered matching logic.
Rooms

Virtual Mentorship Rooms

Students and mentors can meet using proven video tools instead of WHSF building costly video infrastructure from scratch.

  • Zoom SDK
  • Google Meet links
  • Microsoft Teams APIs
This saves development cost, maintenance time and infrastructure complexity.
Learn

Digital Learning Hub

Learners access structured courses, certifications, videos, quizzes, assignments and practical learning pathways.

  • Moodle integration
  • Firebase Storage
  • YouTube private playlists
Designed to complement the WHSF e-Classroom and Skills Passport.
Career

AI Career Assistant

Students can ask career questions and receive guided answers based on WHSF programmes and approved opportunity resources.

  • Cybersecurity career pathways
  • Scholarship preparation
  • Interview and portfolio guidance
Recommended stack: OpenAI APIs with WHSF-approved knowledge bases.
Opportunity

International Opportunity Portal

The platform can aggregate scholarships, hackathons, fellowships, virtual internships, university programmes and women-in-tech opportunities so students can discover and apply directly.

  • Scholarships and fellowships
  • Hackathons and virtual internships
  • University and women-in-tech programmes
Opportunities can be reviewed by WHSF before being published to learners.

Recommended MVP architecture

Launch lean, integrate trusted tools, then scale.

For the first version, WHSF should avoid expensive custom infrastructure. The recommended MVP uses a Flutter mobile app, Firebase for fast backend services, OpenAI for matching and career guidance, and third-party video tools for mentorship sessions.

Phase 3

Courses, quizzes, assignments, certificates, scholarships, internships and partner opportunities.

TechBridge Global can become WHSF’s global mentorship and opportunity engine.

It connects the ICT Girls Club, TechWomen, e-Classroom and international partners into one measurable innovation platform.
