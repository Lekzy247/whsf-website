Who should attend

A virtual career bridge for learners and opportunity providers.

The WHSF Virtual Career Fair is designed to help young people discover technology careers while giving employers and partners a structured way to meet motivated talent.

01

Students and young women

Discover career pathways in software, AI, cybersecurity, robotics, data, cloud, STEM, eHealth and project management.

02

Employers and recruiters

Meet emerging talent, share job openings, promote internships and support inclusive workforce development.

03

Mentors and universities

Host career talks, scholarship sessions, portfolio reviews and international learning guidance.

For employers and partners

Build a virtual booth and meet WHSF talent.

Technology companies, NGOs, universities, donor partners and social-impact organizations can present opportunities, advertise mission-aligned services and connect with learners in a guided virtual fair environment.
