WHSF Events

Technology events that connect learning, innovation and opportunity.

WHSF events bring students, mentors, volunteers, donors, schools, universities, technology companies and development partners together to showcase practical innovation and expand access to opportunity.

Event programmes

Professional event formats WHSF can host.

Each event format supports WHSF’s mission to impact lives through technology while creating clear opportunities for partners, sponsors and learners.

01

Technology showcase

For technology companies, startups and innovators to present solutions in AI, robotics, drones, assistive technology, data systems, eHealth, cybersecurity and digital learning.

02

Student innovation day

A structured event where students and ICT Girls Club members demonstrate projects, pitch ideas, receive feedback and connect with mentors.

03

Virtual mentorship summit

Remote sessions connecting African students and young women with professionals, universities, diaspora mentors and international volunteers.

04

Donor and partner briefing

A polished online or hybrid briefing where WHSF presents impact, funding priorities, technology needs, device donation goals and expansion opportunities.

05

Skills bootcamp

Focused short training events for robotics, cybersecurity, AI literacy, data center awareness, project management, STEM and entrepreneurship.

06

Accessibility and inclusion forum

Sessions focused on disability technology, voice tools, inclusive design, assistive devices and access to digital learning for underserved communities.
