A professional record of every learner’s skills, badges, projects and certificates.

The WHSF Skills Passport helps students show what they can do beyond course attendance. It connects learning progress, practical assignments, digital badges, certificate verification and project evidence into one clear learner pathway.

Accessibility by design

Built for learners with different abilities and devices.

The e-Classroom should include voice-to-text assignments, text-to-speech lessons, captions, large text, high contrast, keyboard navigation, screen-reader support and mobile-first design.

Learning experience

Growing a trusted digital classroom for WHSF learners.

  1. Support learners with clear course pathways, progress tracking and practical assignments.
  2. Equip teachers and coordinators to manage lessons, submissions, certificates and learner records.
  3. Issue verifiable digital certificates with QR-code validation for partners, schools and employers.
  4. Expand WHSF training with videos, resources, mentorship and inclusive accessibility tools.
