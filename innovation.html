WHSF Technology & Innovation Lab

A technology-for-impact platform connecting rural girls and young women to learning, innovation and opportunity.

WHSF uses practical technology to bridge education gaps, strengthen digital inclusion and prepare girls and young women for future-ready careers. The lab brings together devices, robotics, drones, AI, cybersecurity, assistive technology, sustainable tech and virtual learning into one mission-driven innovation ecosystem.

Mission-aligned sustainability

Technology that can support both impact and long-term funding.

Revenue should strengthen the mission. WHSF can build ethical earned-income pathways that help keep core access free for vulnerable learners.

  • Corporate-sponsored device drives, connectivity support and branded impact reports
  • Virtual partner rooms for donors, responsible brands, schools and technology companies
  • Professional training for organisations, with scholarships reserved for girls and young women
  • Certificate verification, advanced bootcamps, mentorship and project showcases
  • Grant-funded rural connectivity, solar learning kits and sustainable technology pilots
