Production integrations

Best integrations to make the app useful from day one.

AuthenticationSupabase Auth or Firebase Auth for donors, volunteers, members and admins.
Role-based accessDifferent dashboards for donors, volunteers, members, coordinators and WHSF admins.
Push notificationsFirebase Cloud Messaging or OneSignal for event reminders, campaigns and urgent updates.
Donation integrationConnect the official WHSF PayPal charity link and future campaign pages.
Volunteer managementTasks, event sign-up, service-hour logs, coordinator approvals and attendance tracking.
Impact reportingImpact feed, field updates, donor reports, photos, videos and transparent programme metrics.
e-Classroom accessDirect links to courses, certificates, learner dashboard and certificate verification.
AnalyticsPrivacy-conscious app analytics for installs, active members, volunteer engagement and donor journeys.
Security & privacyPrivacy policy, consent notices, data minimisation, admin approval and safe reporting workflows.

Pilot readiness checklist

What WHSF should prepare before inviting more users.

  1. Confirm the app name, icon, privacy policy and support email.
  2. Confirm login roles: donor, volunteer, member, coordinator and admin.
  3. Test notifications, donation links, contact forms and e-Classroom links.
  4. Run a small pilot with WHSF volunteers, members and trusted donors.
  5. Collect feedback, fix usability issues and improve the experience before wider promotion.
