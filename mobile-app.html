Install guide

Add WHSF Mobile App to your phone.

No app store is required. Open the WHSF Mobile App page in your phone browser and add it to your home screen.

iPhone

Install on iPhone

  1. Open Safari on your iPhone.
  2. Go to worldhsfoundation.org/mobile-app.
  3. Tap the Share icon.
  4. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  5. Tap Add to place WHSF on your screen.
Android

Install on Android

  1. Open Chrome on your Android phone.
  2. Go to worldhsfoundation.org/mobile-app.
  3. Tap the menu icon.
  4. Choose Install app or Add to Home screen.
  5. Tap Install or Add.
